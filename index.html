WHAT MAKE AN F1 CAR

Formula 1 cars are engineering marvels built for extreme speed and precision. Every component is designed for aerodynamics, reducing drag and increasing downforce to keep the car stable at high speeds.

Lightweight materials like carbon fiber, powerful hybrid engines, and advanced suspension systems make F1 cars incredibly fast and agile. Technology and teamwork combine to achieve peak racing performance.
